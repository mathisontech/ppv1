Our Commitment to Accessibility

Paternus Partners is committed to providing equal access to our services and information for all clients, including those with disabilities. We strive to ensure our digital platforms and physical facilities meet or exceed accessibility standards.

Digital Accessibility

Our website and digital platforms are designed to be accessible to users with diverse abilities and needs:

Technical Standards

  • We follow Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 AA standards
  • Our website is compatible with screen readers and assistive technologies
  • We provide alternative text for images and graphics
  • Our content maintains appropriate color contrast ratios
  • Navigation is keyboard-accessible throughout the site

Content Accessibility

  • We use clear, straightforward language in all communications
  • Complex financial concepts are explained in multiple formats when possible
  • Documents are available in alternative formats upon request
  • We provide captions and transcripts for multimedia content

Physical Accessibility

Our office facilities are designed to accommodate clients with mobility challenges:

  • Wheelchair-accessible entrances and facilities
  • Accessible parking spaces available
  • Meeting rooms equipped for various accessibility needs
  • Assistive listening devices available upon request

Communication Accessibility

We accommodate various communication preferences and needs:

  • Large print materials available upon request
  • Sign language interpreters can be arranged with advance notice
  • Alternative communication methods supported
  • Extended meeting times available when needed

Ongoing Improvements

We continuously work to improve accessibility:

  • Regular accessibility audits of our digital platforms
  • Staff training on disability awareness and accommodation
  • Client feedback incorporation into accessibility improvements
  • Technology updates to maintain compatibility with assistive devices

Accommodation Requests

If you require specific accommodations or encounter accessibility barriers:

  • Contact us at paternuspartnersllc@gmail.com or +1-929-443-3735
  • We will work with you to provide reasonable accommodations
  • Response time for accommodation requests is typically 48 hours
  • No additional charges for accessibility accommodations

Feedback

We welcome feedback about the accessibility of our services:

  • Report accessibility issues or suggestions for improvement
  • Your input helps us enhance our accessibility efforts
  • All feedback is reviewed and addressed promptly
